Foundation

12 web questions

  1. Draw a dotted line to split the L-shape into two rectangles.
  2. Find the area of an L-shape formed by a 6 m × 4 m rectangle joined to a 3 m × 2 m rectangle.
  3. Calculate the area of a room shaped like an L: outer dimensions 10 m by 7 m, missing a 4 m by 3 m corner.
  4. A garden has an L-shape: 8 m long, 5 m wide, with a 3 m by 2 m cut-out. Find the area.
  5. Draw a dotted line to split the shape into two rectangles (different orientation).
  6. Find the area of an L-shape where the longer arm is 9 m × 3 m and the shorter arm is 2 m × 4 m.
  7. Calculate the area of a pathway forming an L around a building: 12 m long, 6 m wide, with a 5 m by 4 m corner missing.
  8. A swimming pool has an L-shape: 15 m by 10 m, with a 6 m by 4 m cut-out. Find its area.
  9. Draw a dotted line to split the L-shape into two rectangles (one vertical, one horizontal).
  10. Find the area of an L-shape formed by a 7 m × 5 m rectangle and a 4 m × 3 m rectangle.
  11. Calculate the area of an L-shaped patio: outer rectangle 11 m by 8 m, missing a 5 m by 3 m square.
  12. A flower bed is L-shaped: 9 m long, 6 m wide, with a 4 m by 2 m cut-out. Find the area.

Proficient

12 web questions

Download PDF
  1. A composite shape consists of a 6 m × 4 m rectangle attached to a right triangle with legs 3 m and 4 m. Find total area.
  2. Find the area of an L-shaped garden where the outer dimensions are 12 m by 9 m and the cut-out is a 5 m by 4 m rectangle.
  3. A shape is made by joining a 7 m × 5 m rectangle and a semicircle of diameter 5 m. Find the total area (use $\pi\approx 3.14$).
  4. A room has an L-shape with missing corner; the outer rectangle is 14 m by 10 m, the missing corner is a triangle of base 6 m and height 4 m. Find area.
  5. Draw a line to split the shape into a rectangle and a triangle, then calculate area.
  6. A composite shape consists of a 8 m × 6 m rectangle with a 3 m × 2 m rectangle removed from one corner, and a 2 m × 2 m square added on the opposite side. Find area.
  7. A garden is shaped like a rectangle joined to a trapezium. Rectangle: 10 m by 4 m. Trapezium: parallel sides 4 m and 6 m, height 3 m. Find total area.
  8. A pathway forms an L around a building; the outer dimensions are 20 m by 15 m, and the inner cut-out is a rectangle 12 m by 8 m. Find area of the pathway.
  9. Find the area of a shape composed of a 9 m × 5 m rectangle and a quarter-circle of radius 3 m attached to one corner.
  10. A swimming pool has a composite shape: a 12 m × 8 m rectangle with a 4 m × 4 m square cut out, and a semicircular end of radius 2 m. Find area.
  11. Draw a line to split the shape into a rectangle and a right triangle, then calculate area given side lengths.
  12. A flower bed consists of a 5 m × 3 m rectangle attached to a triangle with base 3 m and height 2 m. Find total area.

Excellence

12 web questions

Download PDF
  1. A composite shape consists of a 10 m × 6 m rectangle with a 4 m × 3 m rectangle removed from one corner and a right-angled triangle of legs 5 m and 3 m added to the opposite side. Find the total area.
  2. A garden is L-shaped with outer dimensions 18 m by 12 m and cut-out of 7 m by 5 m. Additionally, a circular pond of radius 2 m is removed from the remaining area. Find area of the garden (use $\pi\approx 3.14$).
  3. A shape is formed by joining a rectangle 8 m × 5 m, a semicircle of diameter 5 m, and a right triangle of legs 4 m and 3 m. Calculate total area.
  4. A room has an L-shape with missing triangular corner; outer rectangle 16 m by 10 m, missing triangle base 8 m height 6 m. Additionally, a rectangular column 1 m by 1 m occupies the centre. Find floor area.
  5. A pathway forms an L around a building; outer dimensions 25 m by 18 m, inner cut-out rectangle 15 m by 10 m, and a 2 m wide walkway around the inner rectangle. Find area of the pathway (excluding the walkway overlap).
  6. A composite shape consists of a 12 m × 8 m rectangle with a quarter-circle of radius 4 m removed from one corner and a trapezium added to the opposite side (parallel sides 6 m and 4 m, height 3 m). Find area.
  7. A garden is shaped as a rectangle 14 m by 9 m with a 5 m by 3 m rectangular flower bed removed from one corner, and a circular fountain of radius 1.5 m removed from the centre. Find remaining area.
  8. A swimming pool has a composite shape: a 15 m × 10 m rectangle with a 6 m × 4 m rectangle cut out, and a semicircular shallow end of radius 5 m attached to the longer side. Find total water surface area.
  9. Draw lines to split the shape into three rectangles, then calculate area given side lengths (some missing lengths must be deduced).
  10. A shape consists of a 7 m × 5 m rectangle, a right triangle of legs 4 m and 3 m, and a quarter-circle of radius 2 m. Calculate total area.
  11. A room is L-shaped with outer dimensions 20 m by 15 m, missing a 8 m by 6 m rectangle, and also missing a triangular corner of base 5 m and height 4 m. Find area of the room.
  12. A pathway around a building forms an L; the outer rectangle is 30 m by 20 m, the inner rectangle is 18 m by 12 m, and the pathway width varies: 2 m on the longer side, 1.5 m on the shorter side. Calculate pathway area.
