  6. A prism has volume $150$ cm$^3$, cross-sectional area $3x$ cm$^2$, and length $5$ cm. Solve $15x=150$.
  7. A circle has circumference $43.96$ cm. Using $\pi=3.14$, solve $6.28r=43.96$.
