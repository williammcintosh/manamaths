  1. Convert $4.2\times10^{6}$ into an ordinary number.
  2. Write $0.00012$ in scientific notation and state the exponent.
  3. Multiply: $(3\times10^{4})\times(2\times10^{3})$ and give the answer in scientific notation.

  7. Explain why $3.0\times10^{3}$ and $30\times10^{2}$ represent the same number.
  8. Write $9.81\times10^{0}$ as an ordinary number.
  9. Convert $2.5\times10^{-3}$ to a decimal and to a percentage.

  12. A star is $3.5\times10^{16}$ metres away. Express this distance in scientific notation to 2 significant figures.

Excellence

12 web questions

Download PDF
  1. A number is written as $a\times10^{n}$ where $a$ has 3 significant figures. Explain how to convert it to 2 significant figures and give an example.
  2. A calculation gives $4.356\times10^{7}$. Round this to 3 significant figures and explain the rounding step.
  3. Compare $9.99\times10^{2}$ and $1.01\times10^{3}$. Which is larger and by how much?
  4. If $5\times10^{x}=0.005$, find $x$ and show working.
  5. Multiply $(7.2\times10^{-4})\times(8.3\times10^{5})$ and give the answer in scientific notation, simplified.
  6. A measurement is $6.02\times10^{23}$. Explain in one sentence what this means (student-facing).
  7. Write $0.00000000045$ in scientific notation and state the exponent and coefficient separately.
  8. A spacecraft travels $2.5\times10^{8}$ m in one hour. What is its speed in m/s in scientific notation?
  9. Simplify: $(3\times10^{4})\div(9\times10^{1})$ and express as scientific notation.
  10. A value is given as $4.00\times10^{-2}$ and another as $4\times10^{-2}$. Discuss any difference in significance.
  11. Convert $1.2345\times10^{3}$ to standard form and then to scientific notation with 2 sig figs.
  12. Prove that multiplying by $10^{n}$ shifts the decimal point by $n$ places and give two examples.
