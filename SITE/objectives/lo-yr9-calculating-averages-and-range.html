  9. A dataset has no mode. What does this tell you about the data? Give an example.
  10. The median of seven numbers is 14. List a possible set of numbers and explain how changing one number affects the median.
  11. Explain why the mean might be misleading if a dataset has one very large or very small value. Give an example.
