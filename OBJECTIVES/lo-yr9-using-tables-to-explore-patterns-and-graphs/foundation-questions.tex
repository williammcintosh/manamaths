{\LARGE \textbf{Using tables to explore patterns and graphs — Foundation}}\\[0.2em]
Complete each table, then use the pattern to describe the graph or write the next value.

\begin{tasks}(3)
\task {For the rule $y=x+2$, complete the table.\\[0.3em]
\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
$x$ & $0$ & $1$ & $2$ & $3$ & $4$ \\\hline
$y$ & $2$ &  &  &  &  \\\hline
\end{tabular}}
