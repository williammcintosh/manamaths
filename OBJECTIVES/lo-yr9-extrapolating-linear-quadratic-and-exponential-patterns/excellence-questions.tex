\task The second differences are constant. Sequence: \mbox{$4,\ 9,\ 16,\ 25,\ 36$}. Write terms 6 and 7.

\task A sequence is \mbox{$90,\ 78,\ 66,\ 54,\ 42$}. What is term 15?
